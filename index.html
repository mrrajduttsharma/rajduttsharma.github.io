About

Raj Dutt Sharma is a writer and Customer Experience Specialist whose work sits at the intersection of recovery, family, Hindu myth, and working-class exhaustion. He grew up in Connecticut and spent years in New York City working in tech-adjacent roles, call centers, housing, and late-shift operations while writing on the side.

His books circle sobriety, memory, and the versions of you that survive when careers, relationships, and bodies are not moving in a straight line.
